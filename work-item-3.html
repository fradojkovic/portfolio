Benelli M1014 Shotgun
Description

Low poly 3D model of a M1014 shotgun with PBR materials. Model has 9422 quads and uses Albedo, Metallic, Roughness and Normal maps. Rendered in Blender (Cycles).

Details

  • Low-poly

  • 2K textures (materials)
  • PBR ready
  • Non-overlapping UVs
